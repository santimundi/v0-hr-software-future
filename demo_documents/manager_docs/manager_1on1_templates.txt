Weekly 1:1 Agenda (45 minutes)
------------------------------
1) Check-in (5 min)
   - Energy level, workload, blockers
2) Progress & priorities (15 min)
   - What shipped, what's stuck, what's next
3) Growth & feedback (10 min)
   - Coaching, strengths, improvement areas
4) Team health (10 min)
   - Collaboration, communication, support needs
5) Wrap-up (5 min)
   - Action items, owners, dates

Monthly Career Conversation (60 minutes)
---------------------------------------
- Impact review: highlights and outcomes
- Strengths to leverage
- Areas to develop with concrete experiments
- Feedback exchange (two-way)
- Next-month goals and success criteria

Quarterly Growth Planning (60–90 minutes)
----------------------------------------
Inputs:
- Role expectations
- Prior quarter goals and outcomes
- Feedback from peers and stakeholders

Outputs:
- 1–2 growth goals
- Concrete experiments (projects, ownership opportunities)
- Support plan (mentorship, learning, pairing)
